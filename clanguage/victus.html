Description:

HP’s offering towards entry-level gamers, the HP Victus 15 (2023) is a beast of a laptop packing in the 13th gen Intel Core i5-13420H and i7-13700H CPUs alongside NVIDIA’s RTX 20,30 and 40 series GPU. Its 15.6″ screen with 45% NTSC coverage makes it fun to watch media and play games. It has a good battery, fast memory, and a decent port selection. You can’t go wrong with choosing a good deal.

  • Display: 15.6" FHD, 144Hz
