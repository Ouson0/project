Description:

The HP Omen 15 Ryzen 7 4800H is a powerful laptop designed for gamers and heavy tasks. It has an AMD Ryzen 7 4800H processor, 16GB RAM, and a GTX 1660Ti graphics card for smooth performance in games and multitasking. The 15.6-inch Full HD screen with a 144Hz refresh rate gives clear and fast visuals, perfect for gaming. With a 1TB SSD, you get plenty of storage and quick loading times. It weighs 2.32 kg, making it sturdy but still portable.

Specifications:

  • Processor: Octa-core AMD Ryzen 7 4800H
  • RAM:16 GB DDR4-3200 (Dual channel, upgradeable up to 32 GB)
  • Storage:Up to 1TB PCIe NVMe M.2 SSD
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1660 Ti 6GB GDDR6
  • Display: 15.6″ anti-glare IPS display, 72% NTSC Adobe, 300 nits, 144 Hz refresh rate
  • Battery:70.9 Watt-hours Lithium-ions, six cells
  • Ports:1 USB Type C , 3X USB Type-A , 1x Rj-45, Ax AC Smart pin, 1X Mini DisplayPort, 1x HMDI 2.0a
